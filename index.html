The Top 10 Ad Network Platforms for 2025

Curated from expert analyses, these platforms excel in user-friendliness, performance metrics, and innovation. Each entry includes key features, pros/cons, and average rates based on 2025 benchmarks.

1. Google AdSense/Ad Exchange

Rank 1

Google's ecosystem dominates with vast reach and auction-based bidding. Ideal for publishers with websites/apps and advertisers leveraging search intent data.

  • Key Features: Real-time bidding, diverse formats (display, video, native).
  • Pros: Massive scale, easy integration; 68% revenue share for publishers.
  • Cons: Strict approval; high competition.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $2-10 CPM, $0.50-2 CPC.

2. Facebook Audience Network

Rank 2

Leveraging Meta's 3B+ users, this network excels in social-contextual ads for mobile and web. Perfect for advertisers targeting demographics and publishers monetizing apps.

  • Key Features: Lookalike audiences, in-stream video.
  • Pros: High engagement; privacy-focused post-ATT.
  • Cons: Policy changes frequent.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $5-15 CPM, $0.80-3 CPC.

3. Media.net

Rank 3

Powered by Yahoo/Bing, Media.net focuses on contextual ads for premium publishers. Advertisers benefit from intent-based targeting in content-rich sites.

  • Key Features: Native and display; AI optimization.
  • Pros: High fill rates; clean inventory.
  • Cons: Minimum traffic requirements.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $3-8 CPM, $0.40-1.50 CPC.

4. Taboola

Rank 4

A native ad pioneer, Taboola drives content discovery for publishers and traffic for advertisers in news/entertainment verticals.

  • Key Features: Recommendation widgets, performance analytics.
  • Pros: Boosts engagement; global reach.
  • Cons: Content approval needed.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $1-5 CPM, $0.20-1 CPC.

5. Outbrain

Rank 5

Similar to Taboola, Outbrain emphasizes quality native ads, ideal for publishers seeking sustained revenue and advertisers focused on branded content.

  • Key Features: Curated feeds, A/B testing.
  • Pros: Brand-safe; high CTRs.
  • Cons: Selective partnerships.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $2-6 CPM, $0.30-1.20 CPC.

6. PropellerAds

Rank 6

Versatile for push, pop, and interstitials, PropellerAds suits small-to-medium advertisers and publishers in gaming/affiliate niches.

  • Key Features: Self-serve dashboard, multi-geo targeting.
  • Pros: Low entry barrier; fast payouts.
  • Cons: Variable traffic quality.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $1.5-4 CPM, $0.10-0.80 CPC.

7. Adsterra

Rank 7

Global reach in 248 countries, Adsterra offers flexible formats for direct advertisers and direct-link monetization for publishers.

  • Key Features: CPA/RevShare models, anti-fraud tools.
  • Pros: High volumes; 24/7 support.
  • Cons: Less premium for Tier 1.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $2-7 CPM, $0.20-1 CPC.

8. Trafficbets

Rank 8

Self-serve powerhouse with pop, push, and video formats. Trusted for easy setup and global quality traffic, perfect for scaling campaigns.

  • Key Features: Personal managers, conversion tracking.
  • Pros: Quick launches; diverse pricing (CPM/CPC/CPA).
  • Cons: Emerging in ultra-premium segments.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $2-6 CPM, $0.30-1.50 CPC.

9. Magnite

Rank 9

Premium SSP for video/CTV, Magnite connects publishers to blue-chip advertisers via header bidding.

  • Key Features: Programmatic direct, yield optimization.
  • Pros: High-value deals; transparency.
  • Cons: Enterprise-focused.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $8-20 CPM, N/A for CPC.

10. InMobi

Rank 10

AI-powered mobile ad network, InMobi shines in app monetization for publishers and personalized campaigns for advertisers in emerging markets.

  • Key Features: Miip platform, privacy tech.
  • Pros: Strong in APAC; high eCPM.
  • Cons: Dashboard complexity.
  • Avg. CPM/CPC: $3-9 CPM, $0.50-2 CPC.
